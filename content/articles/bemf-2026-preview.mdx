# BROOKLYN ELECTRONIC MUSIC FESTIVAL RETURNS FOR 18TH YEAR

**314 Scholes, Brooklyn / March 27-28, 2026**

The Brooklyn Electronic Music Festival is back. After nearly two decades, BEMF has established itself as a cornerstone of New York's electronic music calendar, and this year's 18th anniversary edition promises to be one of the biggest yet. A full complex takeover at **314 Scholes** with five stages running across two nights.

## THE FESTIVAL

BEMF started in the early 2000s as a platform for emerging local electronic artists and independent curators. What began as a small gathering in Williamsburg's club circuit has grown into a multi-day celebration that draws talent from across the boroughs and beyond. The festival has always maintained its commitment to the underground while expanding its reach.

This year's edition moves to 314 Scholes, a venue that allows for the kind of multi-stage, immersive experience that BEMF has become known for. Five stages means five different vibes, five different crowds, and the kind of variety that makes festivals worth the ticket price.

## THE VENUE

314 Scholes is a perfect fit for BEMF's anniversary. The industrial Brooklyn space offers the raw atmosphere that electronic music thrives in, with room for multiple stages, art installations, and the kind of production that a festival of this caliber demands. The full complex takeover means attendees can wander between rooms, discovering new sounds and artists throughout the night.

## WHAT TO EXPECT

BEMF has always balanced emerging local talent with established international names. The lineup typically spans techno, house, experimental, and the spaces in between. Past editions have featured everything from Boiler Room-style live streams to intimate back-to-back sets in smaller rooms.

The 18th anniversary edition promises to honor that legacy while pushing forward. Expect:

- Five stages with distinct programming
- A mix of local Brooklyn talent and international guests
- Art installations and immersive elements
- The kind of crowd that knows their electronic music

## THE SCHEDULE

**Friday, March 27, 2026** — Opening night, 5:00 PM start  
**Saturday, March 28, 2026** — Main event, full complex takeover

Both nights run late. This is Brooklyn, after all.

## WHY YOU SHOULD GO

BEMF is a New York institution. In a city where venues close and parties come and go, an 18-year run is worth celebrating. The festival represents everything that makes Brooklyn's electronic scene special: diversity, experimentation, and a commitment to the music above all else.

Whether you're a longtime attendee or new to the festival, this anniversary edition is the one to catch. Five stages, two nights, and the kind of energy that only comes from nearly two decades of history.
