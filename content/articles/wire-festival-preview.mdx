# WIRE FESTIVAL 2026: FOUR DAYS OF TECHNO AT KNOCKDOWN CENTER THIS MAY

**Preview / March 15, 2026**

Mark your calendars. **Wire Festival** returns to **Knockdown Center in Queens from May 14-17, 2026** for what's shaping up to be one of the most important electronic music events in NYC this spring.

## THE DETAILS

Four days. Four stages. International headliners alongside NYC's local collectives. The programming spans techno, house, and experimental electronic music, with artist talks, community discussions, and industry meetups rounding out the experience.

Knockdown Center is the right venue for this. The Maspeth industrial complex has the scale and the sound infrastructure to handle a multi-day festival format without losing the intimate feel that makes NYC's underground scene work.

## WHY IT MATTERS

With the Brooklyn Mirage transitioning to Pacha and Electric Zoo still in limbo, NYC's festival landscape has a gap. Wire fills it by staying true to the underground: community-driven programming, local collective involvement, and a lineup that prioritizes artistic credibility over Instagram follower counts.

## WHAT WE'RE WATCHING

- Which local collectives get stage time (last year's community programming was a highlight)
- The experimental stage, which has historically been Wire's most interesting offering
- Whether the four-day format works for a NYC audience that's used to one-night events

**Full lineup and ticket info at wirefestival.com. We'll have coverage from all four days.**
