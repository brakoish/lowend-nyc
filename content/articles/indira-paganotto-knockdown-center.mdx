# INDIRA PAGANOTTO BRINGS ACID MAYHEM TO KNOCKDOWN CENTER

**Knockdown Center, Queens / March 20, 2026**

Thursday night in Queens. **Indira Paganotto** lands at Knockdown Center for what promises to be a masterclass in acid-drenched, psy-tinged techno. If you've been paying attention to the global techno circuit, you know her name is everywhere right now—and for good reason.

## THE RISE

Paganotto's ascent has been meteoric. The Spanish DJ/producer has carved out a unique lane that bridges classic acid techno with the hypnotic, rolling energy of psy-trance. Her Artcore label has become a beacon for this sound, releasing tracks that work equally well in dark warehouses and festival main stages.

What sets her apart is the sheer energy she brings. This isn't subtle, heads-down techno—it's full-body music that demands movement. Her productions are characterized by squelching 303 lines, driving kick drums, and melodic elements that build to euphoric crescendos without ever tipping into cheese.

## THE VENUE

Knockdown Center is the perfect canvas for her sound. The Maspeth warehouse has hosted some of NYC's most memorable electronic music moments, and its cavernous main room with that iconic disco ball is exactly where you want to experience Paganotto's brand of acid mayhem.

The Funktion-One system in that room hits different. When those 303 lines start squelching through the stacks, you'll understand why people make the trek to Queens for these events.

## WHY YOU SHOULD GO

Indira Paganotto represents the new wave of techno artists who aren't afraid to embrace melody and emotion while keeping the energy at 11. Her sets are journeys—starting deep and hypnotic, building through acid-soaked peaks, and occasionally dropping into full-on psy-techno territory that has the crowd in a collective trance.

If you're looking for a Thursday night that feels like a weekend, this is it. The Spanish techno invasion continues, and Paganotto is leading the charge.
