# CARL COX HEADLINES TEKSUPPORT AT BROOKLYN STOREHOUSE

**Brooklyn Storehouse, Brooklyn Navy Yard / March 21, 2026 / Teksupport**

Saturday night at the Brooklyn Navy Yard. **Carl Cox** returns to New York for a Teksupport production that promises to be one of the season's defining warehouse raves. With **Nicole Moudaber b2b Ilario Alicante** and **Viot** rounding out the bill, this is a lineup that speaks to the serious heads.

## THE VENUE

Brooklyn Storehouse has quickly established itself as one of NYC's premier destinations for proper electronic music events. Located in Building 293 at the Brooklyn Navy Yard, the space offers the raw industrial aesthetic that techno was built for: high ceilings, exposed brick, and a sound system that can handle whatever Carl decides to throw at it.

The venue's capacity and layout make it ideal for this caliber of lineup. Expect a 10 PM - 5 AM runtime, giving each artist room to stretch out and build something meaningful.

## THE LINEUP

**Carl Cox** needs no introduction, but I'll give him one anyway. Three decades at the top of techno. Ibiza residency legend. The man who bridged the gap between Detroit's machine soul and the UK's rave explosion. His sets are masterclasses in energy management, moving through techno, tech house, and occasional detours into breaks and acid with the confidence of someone who's done this thousands of times.

**Nicole Moudaber b2b Ilario Alicante** is a pairing that makes perfect sense. Both artists share a deep understanding of driving, melodic techno that works equally well at peak time or in the early morning hours. Moudaber's MOOD brand has become synonymous with quality, and Alicante's Drumcode releases have made him a staple in the global techno circuit. Their back-to-back should be a highlight.

**Viot** opens, setting the tone for the night.

## WHY YOU SHOULD GO

Carl Cox doesn't play New York often. When he does, it's an event. The combination of a legend at the top of his game, a stacked supporting bill, and one of the city's best warehouse venues makes this unmissable for anyone who cares about techno.

Tickets are moving fast. Don't sleep.
