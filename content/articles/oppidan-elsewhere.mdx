# OPPIDAN BRINGS UK GARAGE HEAT TO 99 SCOTT FOR ELSEWHERE PRESENTS

**99 Scott, Brooklyn / March 14, 2026 / Elsewhere Presents**

Saturday night, back at 99 Scott. Two nights in a row at the Bushwick warehouse, different sounds, same energy. **Oppidan** headlined an Elsewhere Presents event, and for her first time playing New York, she made sure the room remembered it.

## THE SET

**Izzy Fielding** is one of the fastest rising names in UK Garage, and hearing her live makes it immediately obvious why. Her production style blends 2-step with 4x4 NUKG, hip-hop, soul, grime, and UK bass in a way that feels natural rather than forced. She describes her sound as "diverse, ethereal, dark, transient, fun." That's accurate.

The set opened with ethereal pads and chopped vocals over skippy 2-step drums, easing the room in before the bass started hitting. By midnight, the warehouse was deep in a groove that moved between dark garage and bright, soulful moments. Her Rinse FM residency clearly shows in her mixing: clean, precise, and with a sense of storytelling that a lot of DJs at her career stage haven't figured out yet.

The crowd skewed younger than the Pegassi night but was just as engaged. Brooklyn's been hungry for UK garage done right, and Oppidan delivered.

## STANDOUT MOMENTS

- The opening 20 minutes: ethereal and patient, building trust with the room before unleashing
- A grime-influenced breakdown around 1 AM that made the warehouse feel like a London basement
- Her own production dropped mid-set, the crowd reaction confirming she's got the tracks to back up the hype
- 99 Scott doing its thing: raw concrete, no frills, just sound and bodies

## THE VERDICT

Oppidan's first NYC show was a statement. With 99K on Instagram, a Rinse FM residency, and now a Breakaway NYC festival slot announced for July, she's on a trajectory that matches anyone in the UKG revival. The fact that she played a 300-person warehouse on a Saturday and had the room in her hands the entire time says everything. Come back soon.

**Rating: 8/10**
