# AYYBO TAKES OVER KNOCKDOWN CENTER FOR A GRAY AREA MARATHON

**Knockdown Center, Queens / March 13, 2026 / Gray Area**

Thursday night in Maspeth. Gray Area brought **AYYBO** to the Knockdown Center for a show that packed the cavernous Queens venue well past capacity. With **Gabss** warming things up, this was the kind of night that reminds you why warehouse-scale rooms exist.

## THE SET

Fresh off sold-out runs in Miami, Chicago, Denver, and Austin, AYYBO is riding serious momentum. His bass-heavy house sound, built on tracks like **"Hypnosis," "RIZZ,"** and **"I Like Dancing,"** translates perfectly to Knockdown Center's industrial acoustics. The concrete walls and high ceilings gave every bass hit room to breathe.

The set moved through bouncy house grooves into heavier territory as the night progressed. AYYBO reads a room with the confidence of someone who's been doing sold-out headline shows consistently. The energy management was smart: build, release, build higher. By 2 AM the back half of the room was as packed as the front.

Gabss opened with a set that leaned more melodic, setting the right foundation without stealing the headliner's energy. Clean transition between the two sets.

## STANDOUT MOMENTS

- "Hypnosis" hitting at peak time, the Knockdown Center bass system doing ungodly things to the low end
- AYYBO dropping an unreleased ID that had the crowd filming on their phones
- The moment when the full room realized how packed it had gotten

## THE VERDICT

AYYBO at Knockdown Center was the right artist in the right room. Gray Area continues to deliver with their venue selections. With Electric Forest and Beyond Wonderland already on his resume, this Queens stop felt like a victory lap for the current tour run.

**Rating: 7.5/10**
