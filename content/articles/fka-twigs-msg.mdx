# FKA TWIGS BRINGS THE BODY HIGH TOUR TO MADISON SQUARE GARDEN

**Madison Square Garden, Manhattan / March 21, 2026**

Saturday night at The Garden. **FKA twigs** brings her **Body High Tour** to Madison Square Garden for a show that promises to be unlike anything else happening in New York this month. With **Tokischa** opening, this is a double bill that bridges the gap between underground innovation and mainstream spectacle.

## THE ARTIST

FKA twigs operates in her own lane. Since her early EPs, she's been redefining what electronic music can look and sound like in a live context. Her productions draw from UK bass, R&B, classical composition, and experimental sound design, creating something that defies easy categorization.

The Body High Tour follows the release of *EUSEXUA* and *EUSEXUA Afterglow*, two albums that found twigs diving deeper into club-adjacent sounds while maintaining the emotional intensity that's always defined her work. Live, she's known for incorporating pole dancing, martial arts, and elaborate choreography into performances that feel more like ritual than concert.

## THE OPENER

**Tokischa** has become one of the most exciting voices in Latin American music, blending dembow, reggaeton, and electronic elements into something raw and undeniable. Her collaboration with twigs makes sense: both artists approach their work with a fearlessness that challenges audiences. Expect her set to bring high energy and unexpected moments.

## THE VENUE

Madison Square Garden is an interesting choice for an artist like twigs. The arena scale means this won't be the intimate, immersive experience of her smaller shows. But twigs has never shied away from ambition, and if anyone can fill The Garden with the kind of atmosphere she creates, it's her.

The production value should be massive. Lighting, staging, and sound design will all be dialed in to create something that works at arena scale without losing the experimental edge that makes her special.

## WHY YOU SHOULD GO

FKA twigs at MSG is a moment. She's an artist who consistently pushes boundaries, and seeing her take on one of the world's most famous venues is worth witnessing. Whether you're a longtime fan or curious about what electronic music looks like at its most ambitious, this is the show to catch.
